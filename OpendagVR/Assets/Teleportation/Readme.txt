Teleportation System by Patrick :)

1. Add Tracked Controller, Laser Pointer and Teleportation to your controller.
2. Create the tag TeleportZone
3. Add the Prefab TeleportZone to the destination you want to teleport to and change the tag to TeleportZone.
4. Add the Prefab TeleportAnimationCanvas to your scene.

5. Add the [CameraRig] and the TeleportAnimationCanvas to the Teleportation script.
